Student Support and Development:

Anti-Ragging

Our institution has a strict anti-ragging policy. A zero-tolerance approach is implemented to ensure a safe and secure campus environment. Students can report incidents directly to the Anti-Ragging Committee.

Student Grievances

The Student Grievance Cell addresses academic and administrative concerns promptly. Students can submit grievances through a dedicated portal or in-person meetings.

Women Grievances

A dedicated Women Grievance Cell ensures a safe and supportive environment for female students and staff. Awareness programs and counseling sessions are conducted regularly.

Technical Clubs

Our technical clubs include Coding Club, Robotics Club, AI & ML Club, and more. These clubs promote hands-on learning, coding competitions, and tech events to enhance technical skills.

Cultural Clubs

We encourage cultural activities through Music Club, Dance Club, Drama Club, and Fine Arts Club. Annual cultural fests like 'Sanskriti' showcase the talents of students.

NSS (National Service Scheme)

The NSS unit organizes community service activities, blood donation camps, and environmental initiatives to foster social responsibility among students.

NCC (National Cadet Corps)

The NCC program instills discipline, leadership, and patriotism among students. Regular training camps and drills are conducted to develop well-rounded individuals.

Student Council

The Student Council represents student voices, organizes events, and ensures seamless communication between students and management.

Alumni Network

Our strong alumni network helps connect current students with successful graduates. Alumni mentorship programs and reunions are organized to foster lasting relationships.
