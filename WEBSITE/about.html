About

Brief Profile

PVP Siddhartha College is a prestigious institution renowned for its academic excellence in diverse fields like CSE, IT, Electronics, Electrical, and MBA. With a sprawling campus of 19.8 acres, it provides state-of-the-art infrastructure and laboratories for students.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to produce leaders and innovators who contribute to society. Our mission is to deliver quality education with integrity and excellence.

Quality Policy

PVP Siddhartha College is committed to ensuring the highest standards of education, with continuous improvement in teaching, research, and facilities.

Management

The institution is managed by a visionary leadership team dedicated to promoting excellence in education and student welfare.

Academic Council

The Academic Council governs the curriculum and ensures it meets global educational standards.

Governing Body

The Governing Body consists of eminent academicians, industrialists, and professionals who guide the institution's growth.

Hallmarks

  • State-of-the-art Infrastructure
  • Highly Qualified Faculty
  • Innovative Teaching Methods
  • Student-Centric Approach

Accreditations/Approvals/Recognitions

The college is accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade and recognized by AICTE, UGC, and other educational bodies.
