Hostel Facilities

PVP Siddhartha College offers well-maintained hostel facilities for both boys and girls. The hostels are equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and productive stay for students.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls
  • 24/7 security and surveillance
  • Spacious and clean rooms with furniture
  • Wi-Fi connectivity in all hostel areas
  • Nutritious and hygienic food provided in the mess
  • Recreational facilities: Gym, TV rooms, and sports

Admission Policy

Admission to PVP Siddhartha College is based on merit and entrance exams. Eligibility criteria:

  • B.Tech: Passed 10+2 with a minimum of 50% marks in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics). Selection is based on AP EAMCET or JEE Main scores.
  • MBA: A Bachelor's degree with at least 50% aggregate marks. Admission is based on AP ICET or equivalent exams.
